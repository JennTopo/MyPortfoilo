About Me
Hi Everyone! my name is Jennifer, but I go by Jenn, Jenny, can you help me? and even hey you! Ok so a little about me. According to my dentist I am 141 years old. (their typo not mine). I have always had a passion for creating something from nothing. I have a BFA in Graphic Design. I am a rare breed I'm a unicorn; meaning I have a wide variety of jobs I have done and I am a 50/50 split or Graphic Designer and Office Manager.
Not only do I enjoy design on the computer but I enjoy design and sculpting food. I have animal sat all different pets, and for a few years, made designer silk scarves from start to finish. I just moved to CT almost a year ago. I love anything art; painting, sculpting, photography, graphic design, web design...etc. I enjoy mysteries and solving puzzles and problems; I'm always up for a challenge. I have the most wonderful and supportive husband and son. After viewing my array of talents. I look forward to working with you soon.
